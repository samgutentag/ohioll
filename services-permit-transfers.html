Services for current Ohio liquor permit transfer applicants:

Flat Fee Services

I will prepare all documents necessary to apply for a transfer of an Ohio liquor permit, including:

  • Negotiate and prepare asset purchase agreements.
  • Prepare all documents related to the application for a transfer of a liquor permit, including:
    • Liquor Permit Transfer application
    • Corporate Disclosure forms
    • Personal History forms
    • Tenancy forms
    • Source of Funds forms
    • All other required documents

Assembling and Filing Documents

I will also assemble and file your application documents with the Ohio Division of Liquor Control, and then:

  • Monitor the processing of your application by the state, reviewing and responding to all status reports from the Division of Liquor Control.
  • Monitor local government review of the application.
  • Represent you at any hearings on any objections to the issuance of your liquor permit.
  • Represent you at any hearing denying the issuance of your liquor permit.

Among Other Flat-Fee Services I Offer

  • Represent new liquor permit applicants on the formation of partnerships, limited liability companies and corporations.
  • Assist in locating liquor permits when permits are not available from the State of Ohio.
  • Represent new permit applicants in lease negotiations.
