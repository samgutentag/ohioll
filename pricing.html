Pricing

As a former restaurant and bar owner, I know the importance of keeping costs in line. That is why a majority of my legal services are provided on a flat-fee, a fee agreed to by you and me ahead of time.

There are no hidden fees or costs.

You never pay extra for my drive time to or within Columbus. My office is in the heart of downtown Columbus, just two blocks from the Ohio Liquor Commission and minutes away from the Ohio Division of Liquor Control. It is also near the Franklin County, Ohio Courts, where all appeals from the decisions of the Ohio Liquor Commission are heard. Also, you never pay extra for time spent waiting at the Ohio Liquor Commission for your case to be heard. It is included in the flat fee.

To me, part of being a good lawyer is being cost-effective for my clients. Legal expertise at a fair, honest price — that is what I can provide as your Ohio Liquor Lawyer.
